Information for Transfer Students

First we want to thank you on your decision to transfer to Washington University. We know that the transfer process is long and can be filled with uncertainties. We want to help you know that you've made the right choice, and to guide you in a smooth and ultimately rewarding transition into becoming a part of our community. If you have questions, feel free to look at our Transfer FAQs here.
