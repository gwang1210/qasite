Freshmen FAQs

What is residential life at WashU like for Freshmen?
As A freshmen, you will most likely live on the South 40 in a residential college. You will either live in a modern or traditional. You can choose to live in a single, double, or triple, and you can opt for suite-style living. You will have WUSAs, or sophomore mentors, and Residential Advisors to help you get settled in.

Do a lot of people go Greek? Is it a big part of social life?
About 30% of students choose to go Greek. It can be a big part of your social life, or it can not. It truly is what you make it and want it to be.

Does WashU really have tempurpedic beds? Like, forreal?
For real. WashU does have tempurpedic matresses. Enjoy!
