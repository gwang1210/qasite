Transfer FAQs

Where exactly do transfer students live?
Transfer students are generally older, and are often placed in an off-campus apartment. However, transfers also have the option of living on the South 40 to be closer to campus.

Can I fit in well as a transfer?
You will most likely have to adjust to the atmosphere, but generally transfer students fit right in. It usually makes no difference whether ornot you were a freshman here.

Is there a special orientation for transfer students?
There is an earlier orientation for older students, i.e. non-freshmen. You will likely arrive on campus early and join the international students and exchange students for a more thorough orientation.
